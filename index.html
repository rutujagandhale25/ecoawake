What We Do

Learn

Articles, videos and facts about climate change and eco-friendly living to educate and inform.

Challenges

Monthly eco-challenges like “No Plastic Week” or “Plant a Tree Day” to encourage real action.

Share

Upload photos of your personal eco-actions and inspire others in the community.

Resources

Practical tips, DIY projects, and educational tools to make green living easy.

Campaign Activities

  • Tree plantation drives
  • Beach / park clean-ups
  • Online eco-quiz and contests
  • Awareness posters and short reels on social media
  • Collaboration with schools and NGOs

Expected Outcomes

• Increased awareness among students and youth.

• Adoption of sustainable practices in daily life.

• Reduction in plastic and waste usage.

• Community participation and long-term eco habits.
