Everyday Eco Tips

Use Reusable Items

Carry a reusable water bottle, coffee cup and cloth shopping bag to reduce single-use plastics.

Save Water

Turn off taps when brushing, fix leaks, and collect rainwater for gardening.

Save Energy

Switch to LED bulbs, unplug chargers, and use natural light wherever possible.

Reduce & Recycle

Follow local recycling rules and opt for products with minimal packaging.

Plant Trees

Plant native trees and support local tree-planting drives.

Plan Meals

Plan shopping and meals to avoid food waste; compost organic leftovers if possible.

Get Involved

Join our monthly challenges and upload your action photos via the Contact page — inspire your peers!
