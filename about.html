Introduction

Environmental awareness means understanding the fragility of our environment and the importance of protecting it.

Our goal is to educate people about sustainable living and encourage eco-friendly habits. Digital platforms and websites can spread awareness faster and reach more people.

Project Snapshot

  • Name: EcoSpark
  • Tagline: “Small Acts, Big Impact”
  • Platform: Website + Social Media Campaign
  • Objective: To inspire students and communities to take small steps for environmental sustainability.

Key Sections

Learn: Articles, videos and facts about climate change and eco-friendly living.
Challenges: Monthly eco-challenges like “No Plastic Week” or “Plant a Tree Day.”
Share: Upload photos of personal eco-actions.
Resources: Tips, DIY projects, educational content.

Campaign Activities

  • Tree plantation drives
  • Beach / park clean-ups
  • Online eco-quiz and contests
  • Awareness posters and short reels on social media
  • Collaboration with schools and NGOs

Expected Outcomes

  1. Increased awareness among students and youth.
  2. Adoption of sustainable practices in daily life.
  3. Reduction in plastic and waste usage.
  4. Community participation and long-term eco habits.
